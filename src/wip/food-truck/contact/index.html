Our Story

Benny’s Belgians was born from a love of Belgian cuisine and a desire to create something special for the community. What started as a simple idea has grown into a beloved local food truck, known for its mouthwatering waffles and warm, friendly service. Benny’s dedication to quality and flavor is evident in every waffle we serve, making us a favorite stop for breakfast, lunch, and dessert.
